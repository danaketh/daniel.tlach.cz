## Method

1. Toast dried chillies → soak 20 min → blend into paste.
2. Brown beef aggressively in batches.
3. Add chilli paste → cook until dark and fragrant.
4. Add spices → bloom.
5. Add stock → very low simmer.
6. Simmer 2–3 hours uncovered.
7. Add masa slurry in last 20 minutes.
8. Rest 30 minutes before serving.
