## Method

1. Brown beef.
2. Sauté onion and bell pepper.
3. Add garlic.
4. Add spices → bloom.
5. Add tomato paste → caramelize.
6. Add tomatoes and stock.
7. Simmer 45–60 minutes.
8. Add beans for the last 20 minutes.
