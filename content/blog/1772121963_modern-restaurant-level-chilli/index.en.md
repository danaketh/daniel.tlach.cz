## Method

1. Braise short ribs 3 hours → shred.
2. Brown ground beef deeply.
3. Sweat onion slowly (no color).
4. Add chilli paste → reduce until thick.
5. Add tomato paste → cook dark.
6. Add stock → reduce.
7. Fold in shredded ribs.
8. Add cocoa, espresso and umami elements.
9. Long gentle reduction until glossy.
10. Finish with vinegar and precise salt adjustment.
